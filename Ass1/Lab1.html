About me and my hobby

My name is David, and I got into video editing since it was a dream of mine from when i was a little kid to make videos on youtube.

The first video i made was a video of me and one of my friends playing a game.

It took about 2 weeks to edit the whole video and i loved every second of it.

Since then, i have been looking for any kind of opportunity to get me to work on videos in between my busy schedule

I use the free version of DaVinci Resolve because one of my favourite youtubers and role model Markiplier praised it.
And i said, "Why not give it a shot, it might go bigger than Adobe, so I might aswell learn it now!"

Why I use DaVinci Resolve

DaVinci Resolve is an incredible piece of software.
Unlike other programs that make you switch between different apps for editing, audio, and effects, Resolve has everything built into one single program.
It is mind-blowing that the free version offers features actually used by Hollywood professionals!

Fun Fact: DaVinci Resolve actually started out purely as a specialized color grading software for major blockbuster movies before they added all the standard video editing features.

To edit faster in Resolve, I had to memorize a lot of hotkeys. Here are the ones I use the most:

Keyboard Shortcut What it does
Ctrl + B Blade Tool (Cuts the video clip where the timeline cursor is positioned)
Delete key Ripple Delete (Deletes a clip and automatically closes the gap)
Ctrl + Z Undo
